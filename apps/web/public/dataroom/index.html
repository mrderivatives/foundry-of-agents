DEAL OVERVIEW

Deal Overview

▸ SUMMARYAGENT FORGE · SEED ROUND

Agent Forge is the AI-agent subsidiary of TenX Protocols Inc. (TSXv: TNX), a publicly-traded crypto staking infrastructure company. The subsidiary is raising a $3M seed round to build a platform where individuals can deploy autonomous AI agents that hold their own crypto wallets, execute real tasks, and generate income on the user's behalf.

Two converging forces drive demand: fear (40% of enterprise software will embed AI agents by end of 2026 — workers need tools to augment, not be replaced) and greed ($3–5T projected agent-mediated commerce by 2030 per McKinsey). Agent Forge sits at the intersection of crypto-native wallets and autonomous AI execution — the rails were built in early 2026 (Coinbase Agentic Wallets, x402, ERC-8004).

TenX Protocols commits $500K as lead investor, demonstrating parent-company conviction. Investors receive three layered upsides: (1) direct equity in Agent Forge, (2) pro-rata rights to any tokens the subsidiary creates, and (3) warrants on parent TSXv: TNX struck at $1.00 (currently trading ~$0.17 — material upside if parent appreciates).

BUSINESS MODEL

Multiple Revenue Streams

Four revenue lines that scale with usage: platform subscription (Free / Pro $29/mo / Enterprise $99/mo), 1–3% transaction fees, a 20% marketplace cut on third-party agents, and anonymized agent-interaction data licensing to AI labs.

▸ REVENUE STREAMSFOUR PRIMARY LINES
Platform Subscription
Monthly SaaS tiers: Free, Pro $29/mo, Enterprise $99/mo. Access to agent deployment, dashboard, and marketplace.
Transaction Fees
1–3% fee on all agent-executed transactions, trades, and earnings processed through the platform.
Agent Marketplace
Revenue share with third-party agent creators. 20% platform fee on marketplace agent sales and subscriptions.
Anonymized Agent Interaction Data
Intent-to-action pairs, reasoning traces, and outcome-labeled sessions — licensed to AI labs and Web3 datanets for domain-specific model fine-tuning.
